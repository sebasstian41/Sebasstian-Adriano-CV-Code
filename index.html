Book

Poetry Book Publication

Biblioservices San juan, pr
Reading was a key component of my socio-emotional development. It helped me understand the flaws of our world by experiencing the suffering of other people as I lived their stories. Early in my literary journey, I began to explore poetry. Little did I know that, with love and dedication, I would become a great poet. As I dived into a world where heart and mind reflected the essence of humans, instead of any physical or intellectual trait, I developed a passion for human rights and equality. Furthermore, poetry showed me the power that each individual has to change the world through their ideas. Two years ago, I began an endeavor to make my voice heard through a published poetry book. On July 22, 2020, my book was finally published. “Cartas en Mano, Dosis de Tinta” is a poetry book written in Spanish that explores the depths of love and pain through a story as romantic as tragic.
